Building ML algorithms and understanding their impact

Machine learning algorithms are integrated into our daily lives, but what impact do these algorithms have on us as individuals and on society as a whole? Answering this question requires expertise in both the underlying machine learning algorithms and the vibrant, real-world markets with which they interact. I seek to understand and characterize the impact of ML algorithms on people, both individually and societally, and to develop new models and algorithms to improve not only companies' objectives, but also individual well-being and societal welfare.
